The Game of Hip was devised by Martin Gardner, a prominent mathematician who passed away on May 22, 2010. We created this HTML5/JavaScript version of the game in memorial, as part of the Oxford Geek Jam on May 23, 2010.

Check out the mobile version as well!

Developed using HTML5, Raphael.js and jQTouch.

How to Play

The Game of Hip, by Martin Gardner, is played on a 6Ã—6 board. Red and Blue alternate placing a stone of their color on the board. The first person to complete a square of their color, in any orientation, loses. Ignoring rotations and reflections, there is a unique tie game.
